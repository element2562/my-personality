Campaigner: What's that?

Campaigners are greatly affected by interactions with their peers. They are typically the life of the party and are very open minded individuals. They are energized by being around people and enjoy any social interaction.

Strengths and Weaknesses

  • Curious
  • Observant
  • Energetic and Enthusiastic
  • Excellent communicators
  • Know how to relax
  • Very popular and friendly

How to Approach a Campaigner??

  • Respect their ideas!
  • Don't be mean!
  • Remember you have the same goal!
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help!
  • Remember team work makes the dream work!
